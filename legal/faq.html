If I don't already have a website with MAVAJ SUN CO, how do I transfer my website to MAVAJ SUN CO with no downtime?

Transferring a website to MAVAJ SUN CO is simple and fast. Here are the 4 basic steps to do so:

  1. Make sure not to cancel your old web hosting account right away.
  2. Set up your email addresses on our servers to match what you had previously.
  3. Upload your site's files into your new /public_html directory. As your domain name is not yet connected to your MAVAJ SUN CO account, you will need to use the IP address provided in your Welcome email.
  4. Change the DNS settings with your registrar. You can also request a domain transfer at sales@MavajSunCo.com.

Done! If you need more help? Please open a support ticket through your MAVAJ SUN CO Dashboard.
